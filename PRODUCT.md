# AI Solution

## Register
brand

## Product purpose
Marketing website untuk studio AI kecil dari Kota Bharu, Kelantan. Tujuan: dapatkan client pertama dengan menunjukkan kebolehan teknikal + personality yang jujur (bukan corporate AI buzzword machine).

## Users
- Pemilik perniagaan kecil-sederhana (SME) Malaysia yang dengar pasal "AI" tapi tak tahu macam mana nak start
- Penggemar tech yang penat dengan slide deck consultant generic
- Operator perniagaan yang ada masalah specific — bukan curious browser

Mereka cari: bukti seseorang faham masalah mereka, bukan janji-janji enterprise. Mereka skeptical pasal AI hype.

## Brand & Tone

**Voice**: Honest, casual Manglish/BM, anti-corporate. Pakai "ko" bukan "anda". Boleh sarcastic sikit pasal AI buzzwords. Tak overpromise.

**Personality keywords**: Workshop, mechanical, precise, witty, local (Kelantanese sensibility), self-deprecating but technically competent.

**Anti-references** (jangan jadi macam):
- Stripe, Linear (terlalu silicon valley boutique)
- Salesforce/IBM (terlalu enterprise corporate)
- Standard AI agency template (purple gradient, robot icon, "transform your business")
- ChatGPT-written copy yang vague ("intelligent solutions", "next-generation")

**References yang OK** (boleh inspire, tapi bukan copy):
- BikeBear (bikebear.com.my) — local Malaysia, personality, mascot
- Are.na, Index of /Now — editorial/zine vibe
- Read.cv — beautiful Instrument Serif typography
- Old Malay government forms / typewriter aesthetic
- Engineering blueprint / technical drawing

## Strategic principles

1. **Honest about being new** — Jangan pretend ada 500 clients. Cakap terus "studio kecik baru start, cari client pertama"
2. **Show, don't tell** — Bukan letak fake testimonials. Letak actual code snippet, case study walaupun small, atau breakdown teknikal pasal AI yang sebenarnya jalan
3. **Local context wins** — Mention Kota Bharu, Bahasa Melayu casual, Malaysian SME problems specifically (warung, kedai runcit, klinik desa) bukan "Fortune 500 logistics"
4. **Anti-pretty-demo** — Robot mascot yang kelakar/character, bukan abstract glow orb. Marquee dengan slogan jujur, bukan "Powering tomorrow"
5. **Bold typography over images** — Tak ada budget photoshoot, jadi typography kena buat heavy lifting

## Anti-patterns to refuse

- Gradient text on headlines
- Generic AI iconography (brain with circuits, nodes, abstract waves)
- Fake stats (X+ clients, Y% accuracy)
- Buzzword paragraphs ("leverage cutting-edge AI to transform...")
- Corporate "leadership team" sections with stock photos
- "Trusted by [logos]" without real logos
- 6 service cards yang sama format (ML, NLP, CV, ...) seperti template

## Differentiation hook

**One thing visitor mesti ingat**: AI Solution adalah studio kecik dari Kota Bharu yang berani cakap "AI ini ya, AI itu tak" — bukan jual hype. Robot mascot dengan personality + bold serif typography + honest Manglish copy = combination yang takde studio AI lain di Malaysia buat sekarang.

## Committed visual direction (locked)

**Reference physical object**: 1970s industrial machinery manual, workshop control panel, hi-vis safety signage.

**Aesthetic lane**: Industrial-utility — NOT editorial-typographic. Strict modular grid like a control panel, not magazine spread.

**Typography**:
- Display + body: **Bricolage Grotesque** (variable, sharp, characterful)
- Mono technical labels: **Geist Mono**
- No serif fonts. Italic only as inflection, never display.
- ≥1.25 scale ratio between hierarchy steps.

**Color strategy**: Committed — hi-vis safety orange carries 40-60% of surfaces.
- Primary: `oklch(70% 0.19 45)` — hi-vis industrial orange
- Surface: `oklch(96% 0.008 70)` — warm off-white tinted toward orange
- Ink: `oklch(18% 0.015 50)` — near-black, slight warm
- Caution accent: `oklch(82% 0.16 95)` — safety yellow
- NO sage green, NO ink red. Replace existing palette entirely.

**Layout**: Strict 12-column grid visible. Numbered section IDs like `§ 01.02 / KEUPAYAAN`. Modular blocks. Asymmetric within the grid, never centered-stack-template.

**Voice**: Direct, declarative, technical. Imperatives ("LIHAT", "BACA"). Sentence fragments. Reference SI units, model numbers, technical specs invented for personality.

**Mascot**: Robot redesigned — angular, mechanical, less cartoon. Looks like a workshop tool, not a Pixar character.
